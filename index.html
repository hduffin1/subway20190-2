ECL Liverpool

As a destination Liverpool offers fantastic sights, events and activities all packed into a vibrant and exciting city.

With an integrated 4* Pullman Hotel and direct connection to the existing facilities at ACC Liverpool, Exhibition Centre Liverpool offers traders and consumers great accessibility in order to enhance their experience.

Getting there

Exhibition Centre Liverpool is a short taxi- or bus-ride away from Liverpool Lime Street station. There are direct services to London from here that take little over two hours.
The UK motorway network is just 20 minutes away. With Exhibition Centre Liverpool being adjacent to Liverpool’s main through-road, it is perfectly placed if arriving by car.
The 1,600-space multi-storey Liverpool Waterfront Car Park is an integral part of the site campus and just a few minutes’ walk from Exhibition Centre Liverpool. It is fully secure and open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
